I'm a designer and developer with a passion for building tools that make people's working lives easier. I thrive at the intersection of design and engineering — comfortable in Figma one moment and deep in a TypeScript codebase the next.

My work spans UX research, interaction design, and full-stack development. I particularly enjoy tackling complex scheduling, logistics, and data-heavy domains where thoughtful design can have an outsized impact on real teams.

When I'm not designing or coding, I'm probably sketching ideas, exploring type and colour systems, or contributing to open-source tools.
